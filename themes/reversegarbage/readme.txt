Posts
=====
- All posts must be assigned a category of either Donate, Education, Event, Shop
- The news section is the blog roll aggregate of all posts within the site
- The feature category should be assigned to all items that should be specifically highlighted on the section pages for Donate, Education, Event, Shop
- The home category should be assigned to all items that should be specifically highlighted on the home page


Meta attributes
===============
header-class: adds value to class of the h1/h2 heading for pages
redirection (through redirect by custom field plugin): replaces URL to page with value

Plugins
=======
Simply Exclude - used to exclude any particular pages/tags/categories from the search
