Posts
=====
- All posts must be assigned a category of either Donate, Education, Event, Shop
- The news section is the blog roll aggregate of all posts within the site
- Section features must have a custom field of "feature" with a value of "yes" and they will be specifically highlighted on the section pages for Donate, Education, Event, Shop
- Home features are randomly selected from the section features (this can be changed to be explicit about what should appear on the home page)


Meta attributes
===============
header-class: adds value to class of the h1/h2 heading for pages
redirection (through redirect by custom field plugin): replaces URL to page with value
feature: 'yes' value indicates that the post should be featured on home/section pages

Plugins
=======
Simply Exclude - used to exclude any particular pages/tags/categories from the search
